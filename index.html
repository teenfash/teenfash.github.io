About

“The blood of the heroes is closer to God than the ink of the philosophers and the prayers of the faithful.”
—Julius Evola

Teen Fash Records offers the new artist of today a collective within which to assert some level of individuality in this world of the faceless, democratic mass-man.

Emerging as a beacon of resistance against the homogenization of modern culture, we strive to provide a sanctuary for the spirited few who reject the lifeless conformity of communist traps. Teen Fash is not an "inclusive" platform for artists. Teen Fash Records hopes to serve as a firewall through which authentic individuality can be preserved and decompiled despite the ruins of lowest-common-denominator color revolution.

Today the world celebrates quantity over quality and uniformity over excellence. It's here that creators rediscover their aristocratic spirit, transcending the materialist swamp to affirm higher values of expression and meaning. As Oswald Spengler once observed, "The history of the West is a tragedy of soul in decline," yet within this decline lies the seed of rebirth for those who dare to stand apart.

Our collective represents a spiritual rebellion against the leveling forces that reduce human potential to mere statistical doom. As the radical traditionalist

"The individual is merely a fiction, a mental construct, when he is not raised to a higher, superindividual dimension."
—Julius Evola

We are not interested in shallow individual expression, but in cultivating a heroic, archetypal consciousness that pierces through the illusions of egalitarian mass culture. Our aim is to nurture creators who understand art not as personal catharsis, but as a transformative act of cultural resistance—warriors wielding aesthetic tools to challenge the prevailing spiritual and social degradation of our time.
